DBE Metrics

DBE Plan Compliance % (Gauge Visual):

Shows if planned DBE spend submitted by the contractors is compliant with 10% goal. (IE 100% would mean all contractors spend goals = => 10% of their contract value).

% of Goal Paid to DBE to Date (Gauge Visual):

Percentage of goal paid to date (IE if contractor has planned DBE spend of $100k, and has paid $10k to the DBE to date, this would show 10%)

DBE Plan by Contractor Table:

  • Contract Value
  • DBE Subcontractor
  • Is DBE Certified
  • DBE spend goal
  • Spend Goal as Percentage of Contract Value and if compliant with 10% requirement
  • CUF Inspection status and if had CUF issues
  • Amounts paid to date per DBE.

DBE Compliance Dashboard

For the gauge visuals: If no contractor is selected, it will show the average percentage of all contractors. Selecting an individual contractor from the left pain will trigger the gauges to show percentage for only that contractor.
